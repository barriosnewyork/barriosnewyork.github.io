About

Daniel Barrios writes about migration, memory, and the small ways people hold themselves together. His fiction has appeared in Pleiades, The Brooklyn Review, Action-Spectacle, and Querencia Press, with work that moves between wit and ache, the ordinary and the uncanny. He was a 2022 Under the Volcano Fellow and a 2024 Periplus Fellow, and continues to build stories rooted in Caribbean heritage and everyday survival.
